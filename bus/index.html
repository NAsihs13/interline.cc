Welcome to the Sydney Buses page.

There are a lots of things to do and fabulous places to see in Sydney. Our bus and ferry services are a great way to get around conveniently, safely and comfortably. What's more, our services are some of the cheapest available.


Sydney Explorer will give you unlimited travel in air conditioned buses to Sydney's biggest and best tourist attractions.You can buy a one or two day pass and hop on or off as you please.


Airport Express is the only way to fly. With two routes the Airport Express connects the City, Kings Cross and Central Railway to the Airport.


Bondi Explorer will show you the beaches that made Sydney famous, and the houses and bays of the Eastern Suburbs.


Sydney Buses is one of the largest Bus Companies in the world! This means we have one the largest and most sophisticated Fleets, comprising in excess of 1,550 individual buses. Please feel free to wander around our Fleet Pages and see for yourself.
